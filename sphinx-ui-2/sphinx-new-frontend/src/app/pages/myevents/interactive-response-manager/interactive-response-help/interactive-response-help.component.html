General Instructions
  • You can view all the questions by clicking on the Question Paper button on the menu.

  • The question paper consists of Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ) and Subjective Type Questions.

  • Click to save your answer for the current question and then go to the next question.

  • All those questions that are not attempted will carry zero marks.

  • Instructions Click to view the instructions.
Navigating to a Question:
  •    Click to go to the previous question.

  •    Click to save your answer for the current question and then go to the previous question.

  •    Click to save your answer for the current question and then go to the next question.

  •    Click to go to the next question.

  • Question Paper Click to download question paper.
Answering to a Question: Procedure for answering a Multiple choice Single Answer Question:
  • To select your answer, click on the button of one of the options
  • To change your chosen answer, click on the button of another option
  • To clear your chosen answer, click on the    button
  • To save your answer, you MUST click on the Save & Next or Save & Prev button
 Procedure for answering a Multiple choice Multiple Answer Question:
  • To deselect one or more of your selected choice(s),click on the checkbox of the choice(s) again.
  • To change a particular selected choice, deselect this choice that you want to change and click on the checkbox of another choice.
  • To deselect your chosen answer, click on the    button
  • To save your answer, you MUST click on the Save & Next or Save & Prev button
 Procedure for answering a Subjective Question:
  • Write your answer in the given textbox.
  • To clear your chosen answer, click on the    button
  • To save your answer, you MUST click on the Save & Next or Save & Prev button
